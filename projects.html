Featured Projects

WePriSe Automation – Smart Retail Solution

Developed an automated grocery bag weighing, pricing, and sealing system that streamlines retail operations. The solution integrates weight sensors, pricing algorithms, and automated sealing mechanisms to reduce checkout time and improve accuracy.

Automation IoT Smart Systems SIH Finalist

Bio-Battery – Microbial Energy Harvesting

Innovative sustainable energy solution that harnesses electrical energy from microbial activity in soil. This bio-electrochemical system demonstrates the potential for clean, renewable energy generation from biological processes.

Bio-Energy Sustainability Electrochemistry Published

Fiber Optic Gyroscope – Sagnac-Based Sensor

Advanced prototype development utilizing interferometry and the Sagnac effect for high-precision angular velocity detection. Applications include navigation systems, robotics, and autonomous vehicle orientation sensing.

Fiber Optics Sensors Interferometry Precision

DSLIM – Double-Sided Linear Induction Motor

Led the research and development of electromagnetic propulsion systems for Hyperloop technology under Nirmaan Hyperloop. Designed and optimized linear motor systems for high-speed transportation applications.

Hyperloop Electromagnetics Propulsion Team Lead
